INTRODUCTION & HISTORY

Powering The Future Since 2009

KARTIK MULTI SOLUTIONS is a premium solar EPC service provider based in Nagpur, offering high-efficiency solar energy solutions and service-based work related to MSEDCL.

Established in 2009, the company has been providing professional service solutions in the energy and electrical sector. With a growing demand for renewable energy, the company expanded into solar EPC services to support clean and sustainable power solutions effectively.

Over the years, our deep expertise in the sector has allowed us to deliver turnkey projects seamlessly, from initial energy audits and state compliances to state-of-the-art solar framework deployments.

Our Mission

To provide reliable and affordable solar energy solutions while relentlessly helping our customers reduce electricity costs and actively adopt clean, renewable, and sustainable energy.

Our Vision

To become an undisputed and trusted solar service provider permanently known for highly refined quality work, structurally reliable installations, and profound customer satisfaction.
